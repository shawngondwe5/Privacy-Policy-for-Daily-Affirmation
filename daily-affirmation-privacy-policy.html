Privacy Policy for Daily Affirmation

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

Daily Affirmation ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, Daily Affirmation (the "App"), identified by package name com.aistudio.dailyaffirmations.qrvxpk.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information Collection and Use

Daily Affirmation is designed to be a highly private, offline-first personal reflection and daily affirmation tool.

A. Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. You are not required to create an account or sign in to use the App.

B. Local Data Storage (Offline-First)

Any content you create, interactive selections you make, and affirmations you mark as favorite are stored exclusively on your device using a local Android Room database. This data is entirely private to you and never leaves your device unless you choose to share an affirmation manually (e.g., using the system share sheet).

C. AI Generation Features (Server-Side Gemini API)

When you use our "Inspire with AI" feature, the App sends a secure, anonymous request to our server-side API powered by Google Gemini.

  • No personal details or device identifiers are sent with this request.
  • Input prompts are sent solely to generate inspiring, context-aware affirmations and are not linked to your identity.

2. Device Permissions

To provide the App's core services, we request the following minimum permissions:

  • INTERNET Access: Required to fetch new affirmation rotations and utilize the Gemini AI generator. No user tracking is performed over this connection.

3. Third-Party Services

We may utilize third-party SDKs or service providers that process data independently under their own privacy standards:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app installation, update delivery, and basic platform diagnostics. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Gemini API: Used to power the generative AI features.

4. Data Security

We implement robust administrative and technical security measures to protect your local database and server-side transmissions. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Because your main data is stored locally, securing access to your physical device is your responsibility.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is safe for general audiences and does not collect any personal data. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
